ABOUT THE CAFÉ



This café was established on November 27, 2020, located in Angeles City, Pampanga. The idea came to mind when the owner had this idea of making a bright and happy vibes type of café due to the gloomy era of the pandemic. Although, it is not an ordinary type of café, for it contains more than just foods and drinks. Facilities in this café involve a small computer corner, a play area for children, and even a sleeping area for those who want to get a good afternoon nap after a stressing day. The main goal of this café was to spread positivity and to promote the practices of self-care and self-love.




The café serves a variety of things, for example; lattes, frappes, desserts, salads, pastas and so much more! A wide enough variety to satisfy the customer's cravings. Not only that, but the menu offers affordable prices to cater to everyone! Although the menu items are inexpensive, that doesn't diminish the quality or quantity of the foods and drinks being served.
